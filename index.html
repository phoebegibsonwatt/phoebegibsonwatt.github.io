Exhibition: a forgotten Saviour

Whilst summering on the Isle of Tanara Mor, Phoebe stumbled across a retired red life jacket. Initially she was drawn to this object because of its vibrant colour and intriguing form. The more she considered the function of the jacket - to save lives- the more she thought of the jacket as a reminder of her Saviour, the Lord Jesus Christ.
